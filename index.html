About the Creator

I, Alex J. Fehr, am 18 years old and have recently graduated from high school. I currently work out of central Colorado. I have been creating board and card games since 8th grade, but programming is my greatest passion. I have been pursuing software engineering since my freshman year, taking two and a half years of classes spanning Windows, game, app, and web coding, as well as learning on my own outside of formal education. I am now putting my skills and multiple years of experience to work, striving to bring forward high-quality games to be enjoyed by all. So, without further ado, welcome to my brainchild: Imperial Panda Games.

About Imperial Panda Games

Imperial Panda Games began to take shape in 2016. It refers to the group of products created by me, the Imperial Panda, a hobbyist game developer. These products include board, card, and video games that I brainstorm, design, and create myself. While Imperial Panda Games is not yet an official company and has only one employee, myself, it is impossible to know where it will end up. Several Imperial Panda Games products are in development, so stay tuned for updates!

If you are interested in helping create or test Imperial Panda Games products, you can reach me at imperialpandagames@gmail.com
