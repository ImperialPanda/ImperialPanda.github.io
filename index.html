About

Imperial Panda Games began to take shape in 2016. It refers to the board, card, and video games that I brainstorm, design, and create myself. Several Imperial Panda Games products are in development, so stay tuned for updates!

If you are interested in helping create or test Imperial Panda Games products, please contact me at imperialpandagames@gmail.com

Meet the Creator

Hi there!

My name is Alex Fehr. I am a Computer Science major at LeTourneau University in Longview, Texas, and I have been creating games since the 8th grade. I began studying software development in high school, and I also learned coding on my own outside of formal education. I am now putting my skills and years of experience to work, striving to bring forward high-quality games to be enjoyed by all. This pursuit led to Imperial Panda Games.
