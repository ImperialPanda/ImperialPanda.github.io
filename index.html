About Imperial Panda Games

Imperial Panda Games refers to the group of products created by me, Alex J. Fehr, a hobbyist game developer. These products include board, card, and video games that I brainstorm, design, and create myself. While Imperial Panda Games is not yet an official company and has only one employee, myself, it is impossible to know where it will end up. Many Imperial Panda Games product releases lie just around the corner, so stay tuned for updates!
As a Christian, I believe that all activities, even creating games, should be done for the glory of the Lord. I believe He can use even games as a way to glorify Himself and carry out His will. As a result, my goal is to create games as my example Jesus would if He were in my place.

If you are interested in helping create or test Imperial Panda Games' products, you can reach me through Facebook at my profile for Alex Fehr.

In Development

Pentad Knights: A Beginning and an End

Projected Completion Date: October 2019 (Beta Version)

Imperial Panda Games' first original video game has been in development for over two years. It is the first game in a planned series of seven. It is an fantasy action-adventure game that can be played single-player or, for a great teamwork experience, can be played by up to four players across two computers. Explore and fight your way through the land of Therion as you follow the path of five young men on their journey to rid their land of the evil and war plaguing it. Along the way, you will meet allies and enemies that you will encounter throughout the rest of the series. This game has enterred the Alpha version and is available for Beta testing. If you are interested in joining the Beta Test Team, you can get in touch with me through my email at ajfehr1@gmail.com.

In Development

For Gold, Good, or Glory

Projected Completion Date: Unknown

A new spin on table-top rpgs like Dungeons and Dragons, this game began as an idea for an massively-multiplayer online video game and made its way into a board game. The flagship board game of Imperial Panda Games, this game is one of the biggest projects I've undertaken and incorporates a lot of experience and lessons learned across a slew of other games. It features player-versus-player combat or player-versus-enemy combat for up to twenty players. A powerful yet easily understood combat system and a slew of items and character custimization make for an engrossing experience. This game has enterred the playable Alpha stage of development but is far from complete. Even after completion, it will see several expansion options, including a playable storyline.

Fortica

Projected Completion Date: Unknown

Do you love action, adventure, and Legos? Then you'll enjoy this game. Played on a custom board designed and built by you out of Legos, Fortica is a game of exploration and monster-fighting. Designed for anyone from master builders to those aspiring to be, this game is not only a board game; it is a creative opportunity. The only component of the game is the rulebook: the rest is created by you! This game is currently fully playable, but no one except I can understand the rulebook, so it is not yet termed complete.

In Development

Land of Legends

Projected Completion Date: Unknown

In this deck-building card game, you will battle monsters, collect loot and equipment, and enlist allies to help you in your quest to become the foremost warrior in the land. A game for up to four players, it is a bit less complicated than some deck-building games, making it an excellent introduction to the genre. It is nearly complete, with only a few more cards needing to be added. It may also receive an expansion after completion.
