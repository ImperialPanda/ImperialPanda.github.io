In Development

Pentad Knights: A Beginning and an End

Projected Completion Date: Beta Version - May 2020, Final Release - October 2020

The first original video game of Imperial Panda Games has been in development for about three years. It is the first game in a planned series of five. It is an fantasy action-adventure game that can be played single-player or, for the full teamwork experience, can be played by up to four players across two computers. Explore and battle your way through the land of Therion as you follow the path of five young men on their journey to rid their land of the evil and war plaguing it. You will meet allies and enemies you will encounter throughout the series as you seek to save the world (no pressure).
